Barbering

The Barbering course of study is designed to prepare students for the State License examination and for profitable employment a barber stylist, hairdresser, hair colorist, product demonstrator, styling publicist, salon manager/owner.

The course shall consist of fifteen hundred (1500) hours of technical instruction (demonstration, lecture, classroom participation), written and practical operations (actual performance by the student of completed service on another person or mannequin) covering all practices constituting the Art of Barbering. All students are required to complete 1500 hours of instruction and can apply at the California Bureau of Barbering and Cosmetology at 1200 hours for Pre-Application.

The student will learn skills in the following areas, among them are:

State Board Rules and Regulations, the necessity of disinfection, sterilization and sanitation in preventing the spread of disease, patron protection and proper barbering methods. Also, chemical and electrical hazards and other hazardous substances will be included in our curriculum.

Students will have a basic knowledge in Anatomy, Physiology, Skin & Scalp Diseases and Disorders. Students will also learn basic Chemistry and the Theory of Electricity. Subject curriculum also includes Disinfectants & Sanitations, Wet Hair Styling, Thermal Hair Styling, Press & Curl, Permanent Waving, Chemical Straightening, Haircutting, Haircoloring, Bleaching, Facials (Manual, Chemical, Electrical), Barbershop Management and much more.
