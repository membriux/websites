Become a Cosmetologist, Barber, Esthetician , or Skin Care provider

Barbery

The barbering course of study shall consist of 1,500 hours of technical and practical instruction and practical operations leading you to excel while you pursue your dream of becoming a licensed barber. Turn your creatinve talents into a viable professional career with barbering training.

Cosmetology

The cosmetology course of study is designed to prepare students for the State License examination and for profitable employment as a stylist, hairdresser, hair colorist, product demonstrator, styling publicist, salon manager/ owner, or cosmetologist.

Esthetician

The course of study for students enrolled in Esthetician course shall consist of six hundred (600) hours of technical instructions and practical operations covering all practices of an esthetician pursuant to the Barbering and Cosmetology Act.

Nailcare

The course of study for students enrolled in Nail Care/Manicuring course shall consist of four hundred (400) hours of technical instructions and practical operations covering all practices of a manicurist. Students will cover instruction by demonstration, lecture, classroom participation, or examination.
