Perfecta Scholarship

THE PERFECTA SCHOLARSHIP IS AVAILABLE FOR THE COSMETOLOGY PROGRAM (UP TO $5,000) FOR THOSE WHO QUALIFY **The scholarship is available for award during the following months in 2016: January, March, June and November

The dollar amounts for scholarships vary depending on the “need” of the candidate and the total scholarship awarded will not exceed $5,000 (five thousand dollars) per enrollment agreement.

When a candidate is awarded the Perfecta Scholarship, he/she must meet and consistently maintain the qualifying criteria to remain eligible for funding as follows:

  • Enroll in a full-time Cosmetology Program and complete all required course work in the contracted time frame (or on or before the scheduled graduation date stated and executed on the initial enrollment agreement) - with no gaps in attendance. Good attendance is imperative. Any unexcused absence could be grounds for the termination of the scholarship award.
  • Candidates are required to adhere to a full-time schedule of 32 hours a week, Wednesday-Friday 9:00AM – 5:30PM and Saturday 9:30-AM to 6:00PM
  • Must maintain satisfactory academic progress (including 4 evaluations for Cosmetology – at the 450, 900, 1250, 1600 hour intervals) and maintain satisfactory academic progress at 85%with written tests and practical evaluations.
  • Consistently possess proper conduct, behavior and ethics at all times throughout enrollment – abiding by both school and state board rules and regulations. *The school will reserve the right to terminate a candidate or their scholarship, if their decorum and ethics fails to meet the school’s written guidelines.

*Items containing asterisks will be explained in greater detail during enrollment interview.
** Availability is subject to change at the school’s discretion.

Award Amount
$5,000

Basic Requirements

  • Enroll on the full time schedule in the program (32 hours per week)
  • No gaps in attendance
  • Maintain satisfactory grades
