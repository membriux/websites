Nail Care

The course of study for students enrolled in Nail Care/Manicuring course shall consist of four hundred (400) hours of technical instructions and practical operations covering all practices of a manicurist. Students will cover instruction by demonstration, lecture, classroom participation, or examination. Practical operations will cover actual performance by the student of a complete service on another person or on a mannequin.

Nail Care course offers a substantial training program in a short period of time at a reasonable fee. It enables students to enter the exciting career of nail technology as soon as their training and licensing is completed.

The licensed manicurist will be prepared to seek profitable employment as a nail technician, be a spokesperson for the field of nail technology, or own/manage a nail salon.
